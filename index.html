Bradley Knudsen

Manywaters is the expression of song writer and multi-instrumentalist Bradley Knudsen. Thoughtful lyrics combine with soundscapes, ranging from lush to rarified, in hope that, by the sacred practice of making and sharing from the heart, the listener and artist both might be further drawn into that liminal intersection of expression and experience; wherein the body and soul are transported - as if out of itself entirely into a new, yet familiar scene - with every part in place and every breath treasured as a gift
